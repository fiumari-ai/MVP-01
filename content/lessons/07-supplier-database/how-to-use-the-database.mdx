# How to Use the Supplier Database

**Objective:** Navigate the supplier database efficiently to find your next sourcing relationship.

The Operator Path Supplier Database contains 200+ verified U.S. wholesale suppliers, each manually reviewed to confirm they supply Amazon resellers, have accessible minimums for early-stage operators, and are actively available for new accounts. This lesson shows you how to use it.

## What's in Each Entry

Each supplier entry includes:

- **Name and category** — The supplier's name and the product categories they carry
- **Location** — City and state (useful for estimating freight costs)
- **Contact email** — Direct wholesale inquiry email
- **Website** — Company URL
- **Minimum order** — First order minimum in USD
- **Verified date** — When we last confirmed this supplier was active
- **Tags** — Relevant attributes (e.g., "FBA-friendly," "low minimums," "MAP enforced")
- **Notes** — Specific details about their account process, policies, or what makes them notable

## How to Search

Use the search bar to filter by keyword — enter a product category, supplier name, or product type. Use the category dropdown to narrow to a specific vertical.

**Search strategies:**
- Search by product type: "kitchen tools," "yoga mats," "storage bins"
- Search by attribute: "low minimums" to find suppliers with $200 or less first order requirements
- Browse by category: use the dropdown to see all suppliers in a vertical like Pet Supplies or Sports & Outdoors

## Matching Suppliers to Products

After you identify a product candidate through your research, use the database to find suppliers who carry that category. The goal is to have 3–5 potential supplier contacts for any given product, so you are not dependent on a single relationship.

**The matching process:**
1. Identify your target product category from research
2. Filter the database to that category
3. Note suppliers with minimums you can meet
4. Send the outreach template (from Module 04) to all relevant contacts
5. Open accounts with whoever approves you and request price lists
6. Compare prices across accounts to find the best margin

## Updating and Refresh

The database is updated weekly as part of the Pro subscription. Suppliers are removed if they become inactive, raise minimums significantly, or change their Amazon seller policies. New suppliers are added as we verify them.

The "verified date" on each entry tells you how recently that information was confirmed. Entries verified within the past 30 days are highly reliable. Entries over 90 days old should be verified with a quick call or email before you build your outreach plan around them.

---

**Takeaway:** Use the database as a shortcut to your first 5 supplier contacts in any category. Filter by category, check minimums, send the outreach template. The database eliminates the cold-search phase of supplier discovery.
