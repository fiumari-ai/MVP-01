# The Prompt Library Walkthrough

**Objective:** Know which prompts to use first and how to get maximum value from the library.

The Prompt Library contains 50+ structured AI prompts across five operational categories. This lesson is your orientation — what is in each category and which prompts to start with.

## Category 1 — Listing Optimization

These prompts are the most immediately valuable for most operators. They cover:

- **Bullet point writer:** Give AI your product name, category, and three key features. Get five complete, Amazon-formatted bullet points with benefit-first language.
- **Title generator:** Input your product type and target keywords. Get three title variations to choose from.
- **Description enhancer:** Paste your existing description; get a rewritten version with clearer benefit language and better keyword placement.
- **A+ Content framework:** For brand-registered sellers, this prompt helps structure your A+ content sections.

**Start here if:** You have products listed and want to improve conversion rates.

## Category 2 — Product Research

These prompts help you analyze data you have collected and identify patterns:

- **BSR analysis:** Feed in multiple products with their BSR data and get a comparative analysis of demand signal strength.
- **Competition assessment:** Describe the competitive landscape for a product and get a structured risk assessment.
- **Category opportunity spotter:** Describe your current product category and budget; get a list of adjacent opportunities to research.

**Start here if:** You are in active product research mode and want a second perspective on your data.

## Category 3 — Supplier Outreach

Templates and variations for your sourcing communication:

- **Base outreach email:** The same template from Module 04, formatted for AI use.
- **Follow-up sequence:** A 3-email follow-up cadence for non-responsive suppliers.
- **Price negotiation email:** A professional negotiation request after your first few orders.
- **MAP violation reporting:** A professional email to a brand or supplier if a competitor is violating their MAP policy.

**Start here if:** You are actively building your supplier list or negotiating terms.

## Category 4 — Pricing

Prompts that help you think through margin and pricing decisions:

- **Margin calculator prompt:** Input your costs and fees; get a complete margin analysis and pricing recommendation.
- **Repricing strategy:** Describe your current competitive situation; get specific repricing options.
- **Bundle pricing:** If you are considering bundling multiple products, this prompt helps you calculate the margin math.

## Category 5 — Operations

Administrative and operational prompts for managing a growing operation:

- **Customer service response:** Paste a negative review or buyer complaint; get a professional, policy-compliant response.
- **Returns handling:** Get a communication framework for handling return requests professionally.
- **SOP generator:** Describe a repeatable process in your operation; get a formatted standard operating procedure.
- **Inventory reorder trigger:** Input your sell-through rate and supplier lead time; get a recommended reorder trigger point.

## How to Use the Library

1. Go to **Prompts** in the dashboard sidebar
2. Filter by category (Listing Optimization, Product Research, etc.)
3. Find the prompt that matches your current task
4. Click "Copy Prompt"
5. Open ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini in a new tab
6. Paste the prompt, replace the [BRACKETS] with your information
7. Review and edit the output before using it

---

**Takeaway:** Start with Listing Optimization prompts on your existing products. Even a 10% CTR improvement on a listing generating $500/month is an extra $600/year on that one product. The prompts that take 5 minutes to use pay back immediately.
