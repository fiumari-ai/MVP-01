# How Weekly Updates Work

**Objective:** Understand the weekly intelligence cycle and how to use it without getting overwhelmed.

The Pro subscription includes weekly updates delivered every Monday. This lesson explains what is in each update and how to process it in under 20 minutes.

## What's in a Weekly Update

Each Monday drop includes:

**New Product Finder Picks**
3–7 new product opportunities that passed the internal filter. These are added directly to the Product Finder section. Use the 15-Minute Product Protocol (Module 09) to evaluate the ones most relevant to your category.

**Supplier Database Additions**
Any new suppliers added to the database since the last update, plus notes on any existing supplier changes (policy updates, new category additions, minimum order changes).

**Market Notes**
A short intelligence brief — typically 200–400 words — covering:
- Amazon policy changes affecting resellers
- Seasonal demand shifts worth tracking
- Category-specific notes (e.g., "Health & Beauty is seeing increased competition from brand-direct sellers in the Q3 premium skincare segment")
- Tool or process updates relevant to the operation

## How to Process an Update (20-Minute Monday Routine)

**Step 1 (5 min):** Read the market notes. Highlight anything that directly affects your current products or sourcing plans.

**Step 2 (10 min):** Scan the new Product Finder picks. Run the 15-minute protocol (abbreviated to 10 min here since you are just triaging) on any pick that matches your category. Flag 1–2 for deeper evaluation this week.

**Step 3 (5 min):** Check the supplier additions. Any new suppliers in categories you are actively sourcing? Add them to your outreach list.

Total time: 20 minutes. That is the full weekly intelligence review.

## Avoiding Update Overload

The biggest mistake operators make with weekly updates is treating every new piece of information as something they must act on immediately. That leads to scattered focus and half-completed sourcing cycles.

**The rule:** Take one action per update cycle. One new supplier outreach, or one product validation, or one listing optimization — not all three. Depth beats breadth at the early stage.

## Historical Updates

All previous updates remain accessible in the Product Finder section, organized by date. If you missed a week or want to revisit a pick from a previous drop, it is always available.

---

**Takeaway:** 20 minutes every Monday. Read the market notes, scan the new picks, check supplier additions, and take one action. Consistency with the update cycle compounds into significantly better product selection and supplier access over 6–12 months.
