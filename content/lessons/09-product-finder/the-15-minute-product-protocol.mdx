# The 15-Minute Product Protocol

**Objective:** Make a go/no-go decision on any Product Finder pick in 15 minutes.

The Product Finder removes the discovery phase. You do not need to search for products — they come to you. But you still need to validate each pick before you invest capital. This protocol collapses that validation into 15 minutes.

## The Protocol

**Minutes 0–3: Amazon Check**

Open Amazon. Search for the product type shown in the pick. Identify the main listing. Check:
- Is the BSR consistent with the demand score? (Pull up product details on the page)
- Does the current Buy Box price match the average price shown in the pick?
- How many FBA sellers are currently competing? (Click "New" under buying options)

If something looks significantly off from the pick data (e.g., BSR is much worse, or there are now 35 FBA sellers when the pick says Low competition), flag it. The market may have shifted since the pick was generated.

**Minutes 3–8: Margin Math**

Open the FBA Revenue Calculator. Enter:
- The product's ASIN or current Buy Box price as the selling price
- Your estimated cost per unit based on the source hint and your supplier knowledge

Calculate your net margin. Does it hit your target (28%+)? If yes, proceed. If no, can you find the product at a lower cost that makes it work?

**Minutes 8–12: Keepa Check**

Install and open Keepa (free extension). Pull up the Keepa graph for the listing.
- Is the BSR trend flat or declining over the past 90 days? (Good)
- Is the price stable? (Good)
- Has there been a recent spike in BSR suggesting it is running out of stock? (Potential opportunity — or risk)

**Minutes 12–15: Source Check**

Look at the source hint in the pick. Does your current supplier network have contacts in that category? Check the database. How many suppliers can you reach out to today?

If you have at least 2 potential supplier contacts in the right category, you have enough to move forward.

## The Decision

After 15 minutes, you have enough data to answer:

- **Go:** Demand is confirmed, margin works at available costs, Keepa looks clean, sourcing is accessible. Send supplier outreach today.
- **Soft go:** One or two data points are borderline. Proceed cautiously with a smaller first order once you have a confirmed supplier price.
- **No-go:** Margin does not work, competition has increased significantly, or sourcing is unavailable at viable costs. Move to the next pick.

Most operators who run this protocol consistently find 1–2 viable products per week from the picks. Across a month, that is 4–8 new product opportunities to evaluate and potentially source.

---

**Takeaway:** 15 minutes. Amazon check, margin math, Keepa, source check. Go / soft go / no-go. This protocol keeps you from either over-researching (wasting hours on one product) or under-researching (ordering inventory you have not validated).
