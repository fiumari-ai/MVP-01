# Your Operating Model Explained

**Objective:** Understand the complete cycle of the resale operation: find, source, list, sell, repeat.

The invisible store model follows a simple operating loop. Once you understand each step and how they connect, the whole operation becomes a process you can execute, improve, and eventually expand. Let's walk through the full cycle.

## Step 01 — Find

You identify a product that already has demand on Amazon. This is not about inventing a new product or predicting trends from scratch. It is about reading existing signals: search volume, sales rank, price history, and competition level. You are looking for a gap where demand is solid, margin is possible, and competition is manageable for a new seller.

## Step 02 — Source

You find a supplier who carries that product at a price that allows a healthy margin after Amazon's fees. Suppliers can be domestic U.S. wholesalers, distributors, or brand-authorized dealers. You contact them, open an account, and place an initial order. The minimum order quantity at the early stage is often small enough to test without significant capital exposure.

## Step 03 — List

You create or join an existing product listing on Amazon. You set your price based on the competitive landscape and your target margin. You write or optimize your listing copy to maximize visibility in Amazon's search results.

## Step 04 — Sell

Amazon's customer base finds your listing through search. They purchase. Amazon processes the transaction. You fulfill the order (or Amazon does, if you are using FBA). The money deposits into your seller account on a regular payout schedule.

## Step 05 — Review and Repeat

You review what sold, at what margin, and how quickly. You reinvest in what is working. You cut what is not. You find the next product and begin the loop again. The operation grows with each cycle.

This is the full model. The rest of this program gives you the tools, knowledge, and decision frameworks to execute each step with confidence. The goal of the Standard tier is to get you through your first full cycle — from zero to your first sale.

---

**Takeaway:** Five steps. Find, source, list, sell, repeat. Every lesson from here builds your ability to execute one of those five steps.
